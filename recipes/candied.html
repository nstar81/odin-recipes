Candied Asparagus

A classic steak with a classic seasoning

This recipe has been my go to recipe the moment I found out the magic of brown sugar

If you want to take your meal to the next level this is the recipe for yoU!

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • 1lb of fresh Asparagus
  • 1 teaspoon Ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Sea salt
  • Garlic Powder to taste

Steps

  1. Cut asparagus to length (fit the pan you plan you use)
  2. Heat up oil
  3. Place asparagus in oil rotating occasionally
  4. Once it is to your liking add salt, pepper and garlic powder
  5. Add 1 tablesspoon of brown sugar
  6. mix until desired consistency is reached
  7. Enjoy
